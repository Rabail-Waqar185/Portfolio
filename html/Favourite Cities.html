Lahore
A Beautiful City

Lahore is one of the most famous cities in the world. It is the "city of gardens". It is not a city; it is an addiction. It must have been heard that he who has not seen Lahore has not been born yet.

Lahore is famous for many things and is a very beautiful city to visit.

1. Tourism

Lahore is Pakistan's biggest tourist attraction. The Lahore Forts are one of the most famous sites.

2. Food

Lahore is a town with a rich tradition of food. Biryani is the most famous dish in Lahore.

3. Historical Places

Lahore has marvellous architecture. The most famous historical place in Lahore is Badshahi Mosque.

4. Festivals

The residents of Lahore celebrate many festivals, including Basant, Eid-al-Fitr, Eid-al-Azha, etc.
